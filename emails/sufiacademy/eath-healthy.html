Nourish Your Body, Honor Your Faith

“O you who have believed, eat from the good things which We have provided for you and be grateful to Allah.”

This verse from Surah Al-Baqarah (2:172) reminds us that healthy eating is an act of gratitude and faith. Choose wholesome foods, care for your body, and give thanks for each blessing.
